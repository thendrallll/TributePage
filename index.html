L M Montgomery

Here's some interesting details of LM Montgomery

  • She was born on Nov 30, 1874
  • The years she spent on Prince Edward Island inspired her stories of Anne Shirley
  • Green Gables was rejected by four publishers initially
  • Her personal favourite character of hers was Emily Starr from Emily of New Moon
  • Maud received 100s of fan letters, inlcuding one from Mark Twain, who called Green Gables 'Sweetest book of child written yet written'
